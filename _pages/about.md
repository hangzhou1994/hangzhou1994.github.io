Hang Zhou is currently a Postdoctoral Researcher with Peng Cheng Laboratory, Shenzhen, China. He received the B.E. degree from Wuhan University of Science and Technology, Wuhan, China, in 2017, and the M.E. and Ph.D. degrees in Engineering from Ibaraki University, Ibaraki, Japan, in 2021 and 2024, respectively.

Prior to joining Peng Cheng Laboratory, he was a Project Researcher with the National Institute of Informatics (NII), Tokyo, Japan. His research interests primarily focus on Deep Reinforcement Learning (DRL), Massive MIMO, and Cooperative Communications.

Dr. Zhou is the recipient of several prestigious awards, including the JSPS Early-Career Scientists Grant (2024) and the NEC C&C Foundation Scholarship for Foreign Researchers (2022).
